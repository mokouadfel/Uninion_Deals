How to install and configure your IPTV subscription in the Smart IPTV application (Samsung,LG...)

I- Installation:

1- Your television must be connected to the internet.

2- Go to the Smart Hub. For a Samsung TV, it's the multicolored button, otherwise the House-shaped button.

3- Then go to the app market. This is the place where you can download lots of apps for your tv.

4- You should find the Smart IPTV application in the most downloaded apps.

5- Click Download.

6- Wait a bit then click Install.

7- Finally, you can click Run.

II- Configuration:

1- You must have a url address to playlist file of the type m3u : (http://fournisseuriptv/blabla=m3u?user=xxxx?password=xxx..).

2- Visit the site: http://siptv.eu/mylist/

3- Fill in the MAC address of your TV.

4- Enter URL m3u of your subscription in the URL box.

5- Check the “I am not a robot” box.

6- Click Send.

7- Restart your tv, everything should work.

Important: The application is chargeable (about 5 or 6€). You can test it for a week and then you will have to pay to use it. See in our FAQ how to activate the Smart IPTV application.

How to Activate Smart IPTV App

The application is chargeable (about 5 or 6€). You can test it for a week and then you will have to pay to use it. Here's how to enable it:

1- When you start the SMART IPTV application on your television, you should see a “MAC address” line, write down this address (or take a photo with your mobile).

2- Go to the site https://siptv.eu/activation

3- Enter your MAC address (with the “:”).

4- Enter an email address.

5- Choose the Paypal or CB payment method (the activation of the Smart IPTV application will be immediate).

6- Validate the payment and that's it.

Important: The Smart IPTV application is linked to your television. If you change TV, you will therefore have to buy the application.

Method #2 – Sideload Launcher using a USB flash drive

The second way to sideload an app on your smart TV is to use the USB Drive. If your device has a full USB port, you can install files from a standard flash drive, but if you only have a micro USB port, you'll need an adapter or disk drive that uses micro USB to transfer your files. Here are the instructions:

1- Find the .apk file of the app you want to install.

As we have already mentioned, you should be very careful to only use .apk files from trusted sources. https://apkpure.com a popular site for downloading applications generally considered reliable.

2- Download the .apk file to your computer and copy it to a flash drive.

3- Plug the flash drive into your SMART TV.

4- You should see a notification allowing you to open the flash player to view its contents on your Android TV. Make sure you have a file manager app installed and open the flash drive folder to view the files.

5- Find the .apk file and select it.

6- Now click Install.

7- The installation process will begin. Once complete, you can use the Sideload Launcher app to launch the new app you just installed.
